Delete Account

NumRush · by Cheetah Labs

You can request deletion of your NumRush account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how.

How to delete your account from within the app

  1. Open the NumRush app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile icon on the main screen.
  3. Scroll down to Account Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the action when prompted.

Your account and all data linked to it will be permanently removed within 30 days.

How to request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by emailing us:

Email: numrushgame@gmail.com
Subject: Delete My Account
Include: the email address associated with your NumRush account

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email once your account is deleted.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • Your username and friend code
  • Your profile information (avatar, display name, statistics)
  • Your game scores and leaderboard entries
  • Your daily challenge results
  • Your friend list and friendship records
  • All authentication tokens and session data

What data may be retained

For legal, security, and fraud-prevention reasons, the following may be retained for up to 90 days after account deletion before being permanently erased:

  • Anonymized server logs (IP address, request timestamps)
  • Records required by applicable law

This retained data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to contact you or restore your account.

Additional information

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. After deletion, you can create a new account using the same email address if you wish.

For more details about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.
